DNS RECORDS TO ADD IN GODADDY:

Location: GoDaddy → My Products → Domains → thenetwork20.com → DNS

RECORD 1:
Type: A
Name: @
Value: [YOUR_HOSTING_IP_FROM_GODADDY]
TTL: 600

RECORD 2:
Type: A
Name: www
Value: [SAME_IP_AS_RECORD_1]
TTL: 600

TO FIND YOUR IP:
1. GoDaddy → My Products → Web Hosting → Manage
2. Look for "IP Address" or "Server Details"
3. Copy that IP address
4. Use it in both DNS records above
